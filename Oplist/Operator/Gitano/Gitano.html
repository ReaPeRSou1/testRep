Профиль

Gitano is a mystic from Sami, tarot expert and a great card player. She has never spoken to anyone of her past prior coming to Rhodes Island, and it remains a mystery. All anyone knows is that her readings are very accurate.

Запись 1

A fortune teller cannot tell her own future, but she can read the omens and guide her choices. She has the power to create devastating explosions with her Originium crystal ball, but she must also withstand the backlash.

Запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


Divination is a hobby to Gitano that she does almost for free, accepting fruits and cakes as a token payment. Her clients ask for readings on anything from the future to a misplaced key. Just like any soothsayer, Gitano gives ambiguous answers that require some careful thought to decipher.

Запись 3

Требуется уровень доверия: 150%


Unlike her relaxed composure during a reading, Gitano gets anxious easily and becomes very competitive when playing cards. She is never allowed to shuffle the deck out of fear that she'll cheat. Even then, she knows countless other card tricks, and unless she's caught red-handed, she'll never admit to cheating. Still, some people play with her because they either want to catch her in the act, or see a nervous side of her she doesn't otherwise show.

Краткая Биография

Banished from her community for being Infected, she has made a living as a mercenary in the criminal underworld. She is skilled in matters best left unsaid, and as a result of the base nature of the work has become known as "Scavenger." Since her introduction to Rhodes Island through an intermediary, she has given up her mercenary life to become an Operator.

Клинический анализ

Требуется уровень доверия: 25%

Imaging tests have shown the outlines of her internal organs to be indistinct due to abnormal shadows. Originium granules have been detected in her circulatory system, confirming her to be infected with Oripathy.

[Поглощение клеток Ориджиниумом] 12%
Operator has no obvious Originium crystals externally, however, tests have revealed an abundance of crystals internally. The limbic system of the brain has been particularly affected, and the Operator's personality changes are suspected to be related to this.

[Плотность кристалов Ориджиниума в крови] 0.29u/L
Due to high presence of Originium within the internal organ's, the Operator's circulatory system has a high Originium content.

Архивные записи

Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


Having been exiled by her tribe, betrayed by her client, and endured all manner of hardships, Scavenger prefers to act alone and is wary of others. Moreover, since her personality has been affected by Originium, she has become difficult to communicate with. Perhaps she'll open up a bit once she gets used to working at Rhodes Island.

Архивная запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


Scavenger moves freely in harsh environments and will eat almost anything as long as it won't kill her. She is one of the most adaptable Operators, of truly unparalleled versatility. When given the chance, she enjoys resting in dark corners or eating a few specific foods.

Архивная запись 3

Требуется уровень доверия: 150%


Scavenger prefers to avoid other operators until it's not required for her work, with the exception of Provence. She tends to sneer at those who ask her about her interest in Provence, which is reflective of some sort of attraction. While Provence is considered generally attractive (many Operators particularly enjoy fluffing her tail), Scavenger seems to be interested in something else....

Архивная запись 4

Требуется уровень доверия: 200%


Scavenger once loved a young woman from her own tribe, who reciprocated her feelings. For whatever reason, they did not express their feelings to one another until Scavenger became Infected and was banished from her tribe. The next time Scavenger was able to meet the young lady, she had already passed away and with her the knowledge of their forbidden love. This memory is a keepsake that Scavenger will forever hold close to her heart.

Повышение

Разблокируется при повышении до Элиты II


No one understands why a covert mercenary would use such a heavy weapon for sneak attacks, as it is clearly unsuitable. Despite this disadvantage, Scavenger still manages to operate effectively. When others see her pass by, they typically come under the impression that she is not an assassin so much as an individual who will stop at nothing to eliminate everything in her path. No one doubts her abilities, and as for the actual truth behind her choice of weaponry? Who cares.

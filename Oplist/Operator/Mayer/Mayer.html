Краткая Биография

Mayer was born in Columbia and was a member of the Rhine Lab Research Lab. She is the inventor of the "Meeboo" wire control system. She is outstandingly capable and runs her own workshop called "Lutra." She is currently working with Rhodes Islands to provide assistance with construction, planning, and development.

Архивные записи

Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


Mayer frequently encountered Silence at Rhine headquarters since she was responsible for preparing, installing, and commissioning experimental devices for her. This was up until Silence abruptly disappeared which in turn caused this project to be terminated. Mayer often feels a sense of regret when reminded of this instance but had she known the true goal of the project, she would have pulled the plug herself.

Архивная запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


Mayer possesses just the right mixture of a scientist's meticulous thinking and an engineer's hands-on mentality. If there's any downside to this, it's that she never has enough time. If you don't believe it, take a look in her personal dictionary. The word "idle" is missing.

Архивная запись 3

Требуется уровень доверия: 150%


Mayer's head contains a multitude of, shall we say, volatile ideas, which makes it no surprise when she creates something eccentric. Out of her countless inventions, her prized and most cherished "masterpiece" is "Meeboo." Mayer diligently creates nameplates for each individual "Meeboo" and carefully cleans up its wreckage after it's served its purpose. It is clear that she pours her heart and soul into the maintenance and operation of each Meeboo, despite their mechanical origins.

Архивная запись 4

Требуется уровень доверия: 200%


Since Meeboos take up so much space, Mayer's workshop is extremely cramped, causing her to devise ways to expand it. Despite having a room twice as large as anyone else's, she has managed to run out of space after increasing Meeboo production. Mayer has begged the Doctor to increase her room's size. That request was denied by the logistics department out of fairness towards others. Despite this ban on room expansion, the Doctor nonetheless granted her extra space in the hangar to allow her to continue the production of her Meeboos.

Повышение

Разблокируется при повышении до Элиты II


So far, a total of 80 Meeboos have been constructed by Mayer. 20 of them are serving in Mayer's squad, while the others have been disassembled for various reasons. All available components have been installed on these models in service and are uniquely named by Mayer on their respective nameplates. Of the 20 Meeboos in service, there remains a single one with an unknown serial number that Mayer uses both as a mobile workbench and an experimental prototype. Despite its advanced functions, it is unarmed and useless for combat.

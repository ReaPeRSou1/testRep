Краткая Биография

Formerly the personal bodyguard of Lady Fumizuki, wife of Lungmen's Chief Wei Yenwu, ShiraYuki was sent to Rhodes Island to serve the Doctor in the same role, after the Lungmen Guard Department partnered with the company. She uses large shurikens as her weapons of choice, and is well-versed in covert ops and intelligence gathering. She protects the Doctor from behind the scenes.

Архивные записи

Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


A reserved ninja who is incredibly succinct with her words, never using two when just one would do. You never know where she might be until she is needed by the Doctor and she appears with unerring, possibly even eerie, dependability. Her hobby is apparently watching the snow from the coast, but it is not known if her preference is for the coast, the snow, or a necessary combination of the two.

Архивная запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


Large shurikens are ShiraYuki's weapon of choice. There are many shapes and sizes of shurikens, but no matter the type, throwing weapons are always difficult to wield effectively. Undoubtedly, ShiraYuki is an expert at using them, exhibiting accuracy and lethality that most normal people would find impossible given the unwieldiness of these weapons.

Архивная запись 3

Требуется уровень доверия: 200%


The ninja is commonly held as one of the national symbols of the Far East. These mysterious assassins can allegedly walk on walls, burrow underground, and kill from impossible angles. They are said to value loyalty above all else. A ninja, upon pledging fealty, will serve his lord unwaveringly. And that is exactly what Dr. Kal'tsit is having an issue with--ShiraYuki's loyalty lies with Madam Fumizuki, not the good Doctor at Rhodes Island.

Архивная запись 4

Требуется уровень доверия: 200%


Of course, the true purpose of ShiraYuki's presence at Rhodes Island is not to protect the Doctor, that much is certain. That was just a pretext for Rhodes Island to accept her. Still, that does raise some questions. Even though it's ostensibly a partnership, Lungmen and Rhodes Island hold different views and objectives. Still, the Governor sent off his wife's personal bodyguard to protect the Doctor. Whether for surveillance or a show of power, it was very blunt, even unnecessary. Supposedly, Madam Fumizuki doesn't involve herself in politics, but it's hard to imagine she has no ulterior motive with this move. So then, what are ShiraYuki's orders after all?

Краткая Биография

Haze is an operator from Victoria, where she did time in an Infected quarantine lockup. She finally escaped after multiple attempts, eventually joining Rhodes Island under an alias.

Клинический анализ

Требуется уровень доверия: 25%

Imaging tests for this Operator showed blurry outlines of internal organs with visible dark spots. Unusually high concentrations of Originium particles were present in her circulatory system, indicating infection and confirming her as Infected at this stage.

Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


Haze is able to cast Dark Fog Arts, a spell unique to her. The versatile mist can obscure vision, damage, kill, or enhance Haze's own abilities.

Архивная запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


Haze has a habit of palming things from those around her. While she never steals money or keys, no trifling trinkets are safe with her around. Such trivial thievery wouldn't normally get anyone in serious trouble, but upon getting picked up for a minor infraction, her infection was discovered, so the authorities dumped her into a cell for the Infected.

Архивная запись 3

Требуется уровень доверия: 150%


Her prison was especially built to hold Infected criminals. To break out and breathe free once more, she had to climb the towering quarantine wall and evade all security measures in between. Haze is the only one to have managed such a feat, with the Victorian Police still hunting her to this day.

Архивная запись 4

Требуется уровень доверия: 200%


Haze makes no secret of her fondness for feline creatures. Even though these same cute animals led to her infection, she still feeds and cares for them. So it's no surprise Haze gets along with other Felines like Mousse.

Архивные записи

Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


Matoimaru is a magnificent warrior from the Far East.
She is simpleminded and prone to deception. She does catch on, but a new trick will fool her again.
Her hobbies, at which she excels, are sparring, soccer, and floral arrangement. She has been known to organize events around these subjects, though her idea of organization is typically dragging people kicking and screaming into it.

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


The Oni are still a mystery to most people. Aside from living in the Far East, there seems to be little in common among the race.

Архивная запись 4

Требуется уровень доверия: 200%


Matoimaru is obviously self-conscious about her flaws: She is not good at controlling her strength or at critical thinking.
She took up floral arrangement to refine herself and to show the others they don't need to fear her.
Despite this, she's remains quite gullible. At least she has learned to question the idea that she must be a great thinker in order to survive.
As far as she is concerned, her ideal companion would be someone who won't deceive or betray her, and will stay with her and guide her. How wonderful that would be...

Краткая Биография

12F was the alias commonly used by this Operator in his mercenary work. By his own account, he vacillated between different factions and was involved in several famous battles over the past few years. The few verifiable documents we have confirmed that 12F was indeed present for these conflicts, either as a regular mercenary or security for a local facility. He joined Rhodes Island with Owl, having been admitted for his unique combat experience as well as his experience in general. Now a standard Arts Operator assigned to normal duty.
